1. Follow ButterDogCo Terms of Service

This is self explanatory, you must follow the ButterDogCo Terms of Service at all times.

2. Be reasonably kind to others

We don't really care if you're being stupid, but don't be just rude.

3. Griefing

Griefing is not allowed in the server at all.

4. Impersonation

Impersonation is strictly prohibited.

The primary reason for this rule existing is due to the previous versions of the server, where anyone could log on with any username and make an account.

5. Hacking/Cheating

Any sort of cheating/hacking is strictly prohibited.

6. PVP

Prohibited

  • Once you start you may not stop unless all agree, or a player dies. The player that died may "exit" PVP if they wish.
  • You may not PVP someone inside of their base without permission.

Allowed

  • May be started by a player attacking another player, ensure you follow the prohibited rules regarding this.

7. Stealing

Stealing is allowed with the following rules:

  • You may not abuse any cheats, exploits, or bugs.
  • You may not use any potion effects that make you harder to see (invisibility).
  • The player you are stealing from MUST be online.
  • The player you are stealing from may fight back.

Reporting someone

If you want to report someone, you can do so in the Discord.

Our staff reserve the right to warn, kick, ban, or punish you in a reasonable way.

Any abuse of power by staff, or any member with a high rank must be reported to @k_ws or @AccidentalTheory on Discord.
